Fishing Gear

Spinning Rod

A spinning rod is a versatile fishing rod designed for use with a spinning reel mounted on the bottom, featuring large guides for easy line flow and reduced friction. Ideal for beginners and finesse fishing, they excel at casting lightweight lures (1/16 to 1 ounce) and techniques using 4-12 lb line.

Bait: Worms,Cheese, Fish Eggs, etc

Baitcasting Rod

Baitcasting rods are specialized fishing rods designed for accuracy, power, and handling heavy lines/lures, characterized by a top-mounted reel and a "trigger" grip for enhanced control. Ideal for bass and larger species, they allow for precise casting, especially when using heavier lures

Bait: Heavy lures

Fly Rod

A fly rod is a specialized, flexible fishing rod used to cast nearly weightless artificial flies, relying on the weight of the line rather than the lure. Modern rods are predominantly made from graphite/carbon fiber for, durability and performance, though bamboo and fiberglass are still used. They are classified by length, line weight (0-15), and action.

Bait: Flies
