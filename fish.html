Fishing Guide

Fish Species

Largemouth Bass

Bass are aggressive freshwater fish known for strong fights.

Where: Warm lakes like Utah Lake

Bait: Worms, crankbaits

Tip: Fish early morning or evening

Rainbow Trout

Trout are common in Utah and prefer cold water.

Where: Strawberry Reservoir

Bait: Powerbait, flies

Tip: Use light line

Catfish

Catfish are bottom feeders that hunt by smell.

Where: Utah Lake

Bait: Chicken liver

Tip: Fish at night
